Brief resume

Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Basque Center for Applied Mathematics (BCAM), Mathematical, Computational and Experimental Neuroscience (MCEN) Group, Universidad del País Vasco (UPV), Basque Country, Spain. My research focuses on Topological and Geometrical data analysis for big data, with applications to time-series, protein, and neuronal data, including fMRI and MEG datasets, image processing, clustering, and machine learning algorithms.
